SoftVFD

SoftNet Online portal for taxpayers to issue fiscal receipts

About SoftVFD

SoftVFD is an application system which enable the tax payer issue fiscal receipts with QR Code in real-time. Through the platforms, taxpayers can register products/services, record sales transactions, print or share fiscal receipt with customer, view different reports depending on the selected package etc.

  • SoftVFD Mission

    To intergrate Tax payers and the customers making easy way through increasing tax compliance.

  • SoftVFD Vision

    To build an online platform that will be a better option in making business to different customers through the Tax payment to TRA.

Why SoftVFD ?

SoftVFD aims at reducing the use of EFD machine through provision of a well intergrated portal that will support its uses and the customers in Tax collection.

Cloud Based Solution

Hosted on high perfomance servers for best online experience.VFD system can be accessed from anywhere,anytime

Efficient

Eliminates the need for physical checks, saving time for both the Tax Administrator and the taxpayers

Transparent

Sends real-time transaction data to the Tax Administrator and ensures continuous monitoring of taxpayers.

    Questions? look here!

    The following are fluently asked question and their posible answers

    What is VFD?

    is a new system that uses state-of-the-art technology and is directly linked to the Tanzania Revenue Authority (TRA) to enable a service provider or trader to issue valid EFD receipts using smart phones or computers.

    Do i need an EFD Machine to use VFD?

    No,you dont need an EFD Machine to use VFD Application,VFD is an online solution that can be accessed from any smart device that has internet access e.g Laptop, Desktop, also via our Mobile Applications on Smartphone,or Tablet for Android and IOS.

    What are the requirements to get VFD account?

    To get VFD account one is required to have TIN Certificate, VRN Certificate (if VAT Registered), and an Application Letter.For more details please contact us

    How long does it take to get VFD account?

    It takes only two (2) working days to get your VFD acccount to be fully integrated and enjoy the flexibility.

    Can i integrate VFD with third party applications?

    Definately,VFD application architecture allows integrating it with other applications through its secure API's.
